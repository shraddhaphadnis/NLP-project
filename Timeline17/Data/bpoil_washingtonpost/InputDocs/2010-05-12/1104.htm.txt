Oil spill cleanup , containment efforts , hearings in wake of gulf disaster Cleanup and containment efforts continue at the Gulf of Mexico site of the oil spill following the Deepwater Horizon explosion . 
Oil floats on the surface in Pass A Loutre near Venice , La. . 
BP launched an ambitious deep-sea operation to choke off the gushing oil leak on Wednesday , but President Obama cautioned Americans there was no guarantee it would work .
