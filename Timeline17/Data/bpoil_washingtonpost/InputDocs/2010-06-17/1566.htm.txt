Leeville , La. , tries to weather the BP oil spill The town of Leeville , La. , is home to only a handful of people whose lives are twined with the fishing and oil industries . 
With storm surges swallowing more land every year , Leeville has always been on the verge of disappearing . 
When fishing waters were closed due to the Deepwater Horizon leak , Leeville 's fate was hastened : The fishermen have fled and businesses are suffering .
