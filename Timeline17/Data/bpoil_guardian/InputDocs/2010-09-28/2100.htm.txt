BP oil spill : an interactive timeline On 20 April 2010 an explosion on the Deepwater Horizon drilling rig killed 11 men and sent millions of gallons of oil gushing into the Gulf of Mexico . 
This timeline tracks BP 's attempts to control the spill , the unfolding environmental disaster and the political fallout of a bitter confrontation with the Obama administration .
