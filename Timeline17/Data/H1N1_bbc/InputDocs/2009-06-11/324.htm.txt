WHO declares swine flu pandemic The Director General of the World Health Organization , Margaret Chan , has formally declared that the swine flu outbreak is now a global pandemic ; the first global flu epidemic in 41 years . 
In a statement , the WHO says it decided to raise the pandemic alert level from phase five to six , meaning the swine flu virus is spreading in at least two regions of the world . 
The move does not necessarily mean the virus is causing more severe illness or more deaths .
