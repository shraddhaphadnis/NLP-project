12 October 2011 Last updated at 20:01 GMT Help The legal team representing Michael Jackson 's personal doctor has dropped its claim that the pop star swallowed a fatal dose of the anesthetic Propofol while the medic was not looking . 
Dr Conrad Murray is on trial charged with involuntary manslaughter following Jackson 's death in June 2009 . 
He has pleaded not guilty . 
The BBC 's Peter Bowes in Los Angeles said that the move was a huge about turn by Dr Murray 's defense .
