Leading poets including Roger McGough and poet Laureate Carol Ann Duffy are taking part in a live poetry event in Westminster tonight to raise money for the Haiti earthquake relief efforts . 
Poetry Live for Haiti will be held at Central Hall , Westminster , from 2.30 pm .
