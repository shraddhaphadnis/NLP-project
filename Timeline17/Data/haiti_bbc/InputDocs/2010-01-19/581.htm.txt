Last updated : 19 January , 2010 - 14:10 GMT Haiti earthquake : the struggle to distribute aid The former US President , Bill Clinton , has been in Haiti watching the international aid operation for himself at Port-au-Prince airport .

The BBC 's Matt Frei caught up with him amid the roar of the plane engines and asked for his impressions of the situation on the ground .
