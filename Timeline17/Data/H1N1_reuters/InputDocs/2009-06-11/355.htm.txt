STOCKHOLM | Thu Jun 11 , 2009 3:31 pm BST STOCKHOLM -LRB- Reuters -RRB- - Sweden 's Ministry of Health and Social Affairs said on Thursday the World Health Organisation has decided to raise the new H1N1 virus threat level to phase six . 
`` Today ... the Minister for Elderly Care and Public Health Maria Larsson has called a press conference following a decision by the WHO to raise the pandemic level to six for the influensa A H1N1 virus . '' 
The WHO has said that phase six would reflect the fact that the disease , widely known as swine flu , is spreading geographically , but not necessarily indicate how virulent it is .
