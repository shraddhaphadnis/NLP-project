RABAT , June 12 -LRB- Reuters -RRB- - Morocco has confirmed its first case of the H1N1 flu virus in a 18-year-old woman who had returned home from studying at a university in Canada , the North African country 's health ministry said on Friday . 
The woman arrived on Wednesday at her home city of Fes on board a Royal Air Maroc flight via Casablanca from Montreal and showed the first symptoms , the ministry added in a statement . 
`` Results of -LRB- laboratory exams of a sample -RRB- have confirmed the existence of AH1N1 virus on the young woman , whose state of health is currently stable , '' the ministry said . 
Authorities were providing the required health care to the woman 's family and checking whether any of the passengers who were on the flights Montreal-Casablanca and Casablanca-Fes might be infected by the virus . 
`` Since the outbreak abroad of the virus , Morocco has established a surveillance system and strengthened the surveillance of passengers boarding flights from countries with AH1N1 pandemics , '' the ministry said .
